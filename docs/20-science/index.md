# 20-science Index

## Purpose
Index the governed science ledgers, generated inventories, and per-domain science landing pages.

## Scope
Documents under `docs/20-science/`.

## Non-goals
- Replacing the lower-level domain manifests and generated science inventories.
- Hiding domain-specific detail behind one top-level summary page.

## Contracts
- Every governed root science doc must be linked here exactly once.
- Domain-specific science families must enter through their landing pages, not ad hoc deep links.
