# BAM References

## What
Primary literature references for BAM tools.

## Why
Provides scientific provenance.

## Non-goals
- Comprehensive bibliography.

## Contracts
- Tools in roster must have citations here or in stage docs.

## Examples
- BWA: Li & Durbin, 2009.

## Failure modes
- Missing citations for declared tools.
